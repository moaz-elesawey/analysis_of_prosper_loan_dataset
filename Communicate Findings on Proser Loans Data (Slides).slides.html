Relation between the Loan Amount and Borrower Annual Percentage Rate and effect of Prosper Rating.¶

ProsperRating has a great effect on the relation between LoanOriginalAmount and BorrowerAPR as the overall relation is Negative but the relation differ from one ProsperRating to another as it:

  • Negative for: (HR ,E ,D ,C ,B)
  • Positive for : (AA, A)

which means that the clients with (A, AA) rating when the Loan Amount increases the BorrowerAPR increases.
